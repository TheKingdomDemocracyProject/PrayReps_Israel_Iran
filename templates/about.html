Welcome to PrayReps

PrayReps is a tool designed to help individuals and groups systematically pray for those who serve their countries as part of government. This version focuses on Israel and Iran.

"...for the authorities are God‚Äôs servants, who give their full time to governing."

Our vision is to foster a love for those who serve the world by giving their full time to governing through making it easier to engage in consistent and informed prayer for those in leadership, seeking the increase of God's Kingdom in their lives, their work, and the nations they serve.

How it works

  1. View representative: On the home page, you'll be presented with one representative at a time. You'll see their name, photo (if available), political party, and the country they serve.
  2. The map: A hexagonal map of the representative's country is displayed.
    • An area on the map will be highlighted in yellow.
    • In some countries that area will be an approximation of where that person is elected to serve
    • Locations for representatives you've already prayed for will be marked with a heart icon (‚ù§Ô∏è).
  3. Pray: Take a moment to pray for the displayed representative.
  4. Mark as prayed: Once you've prayed, click the "üôè Amen" button. This records your prayer, marks the location on the map with a heart, and then presents the next representative from the queue.

Navigating the site

  • Home: Your main page for praying for representatives.
  • Queue: See the full list of representatives currently waiting for prayer.
  • Prayed List: View a list of all representatives you've prayed for. You can also choose to put someone back into the prayer queue from this page if you wish to pray for them again.
  • Statistics: Explore prayer statistics, including breakdowns by party and prayer activity over time.

The Vision

This tool is part of The Kingdom Democracy Project, which seeks to enable the Church to love and serve the places we vote in and the people we vote for. While this is a simple tool, we hope it serves as a helpful resource.
