Welcome to PrayReps!

PrayReps is a tool designed to help individuals and groups systematically pray for elected representatives. This version focuses on the representatives serving the countries of Israel and Iran.

Our vision is to foster consistent and informed prayer for those in leadership, believing that prayer can make a difference in their lives, their work, and the nations they serve.

How It Works

  1. View Representative: On the Home page, you'll be presented with one representative at a time. You'll see their name, photo (if available), political party, and the country they serve.
  2. The Map: A hexagonal map of the representative's country is displayed.
    • The area the current representative is associated with will be highlighted (usually in yellow).

  3. Pray: Take a moment to pray for the displayed representative.

Navigating the Site

  • Home: Your main page for praying for representatives.
  • Queue: See the full list of representatives currently waiting for prayer.
  • Prayed List: View a list of all representatives you've prayed for. You can also choose to put someone back into the prayer queue from this page if you wish to pray for them again.
  • Statistics: Explore prayer statistics, including breakdowns by party and prayer activity over time.

The Vision

This application is inspired by the vision of the Kingdom Democracy Project, which seeks to encourage Christians to engage positively with democratic processes, including praying for those in authority. While this is a simple tool, we hope it serves as a helpful resource.
